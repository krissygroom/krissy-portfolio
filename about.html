My Journey

I've made my home base in sunny San Diego, California, with my family of 4 and our 3 cats . My education started in psychology and I continued to explore different academic interests but I eventually landed in computer science. I quickly realized that the areas I'm most passionate about are: beautiful design & intuitive functionality, excellent human experiences, and technologies that embody diversity, inclusion, and accessibility

I started my career in data and AI, however my enthusiasm for creative technologies and positive user experiences led me to front end development and UX. I love visual communication and creativity! I'm very excited to be able to blend my technical skills, empathy, and my eye for detail and gorgeous design to create products and experiences that make a real difference in people's lives.
Can't wait to work with you!
